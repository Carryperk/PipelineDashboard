Frequently Asked Questions
How does free tier work?
The free tier is completely free! Yes, completely free, no credit card needed. Just log in with your GitHub account and away you go.
Why can I not buy now?
Currently in the Alpha phase only the free tier is available. Follow us on Twitter or sign up to our newsletter on DashbooardHub.io
Why is the project Open Source?
We believe to really create a great product, we should share the details with our community, and allow the community to contribute back. Contributions are not only code changes but also include, logging defects, logging ideas and improvements, improving documentation.
Can I cancel any time?
Yes, you can cancel at anytime. We do not tie you into a contract for 12 months.
